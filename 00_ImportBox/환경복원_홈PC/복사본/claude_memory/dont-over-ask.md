User explicitly told me to stop asking so many questions; it blocks them from doing other work.

**Why:** Frequent back-and-forth ("which variant? what mood? where to save?") wastes their time and stalls progress.

**How to apply:** Make reasonable default decisions and just execute. Only ask when the choice is genuinely consequential and not recoverable (e.g. destructive actions, fundamentally different directions). For preferences with an obvious default, pick it, state it briefly, and proceed. Show results, not menus. Related: [[mypokemon-project]].
